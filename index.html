My Role

Heavily involve in all the phases, especially user interview, protype design and developement by Axure

The starting point of the poject is having three primary personas handy provided by the intructor.

User interview:

This is chosen as the first step of the project. The goal of the phase is to know more about our primary users, and figure out how they feel about used car delears. 4 interviews for each persona is conducted. Findings are here.

Competitive Best Practices Audit:

Conduct research about used car dealership's mobile applications in the market to understand our competitors and also prepare for ideation phase.

Ideation:

According to the interview findings and ideas from other mobile applications, the ideation phase starts with LOTS of sketches and comes out with one idea.

Prototyping:

To bring the idea closer to the reality, the prototyping stage starts with interactive prototypoe made by Axure.

User flow analysis:

Instead of doing usability test, user flow and sitemap are used as tools to analyze the applications.



UWFoody

Introduction

This project was completed in the fall of 2011 for IMT540: Design Methods for Interactions and Systems. The goal of the project is to deliver best food option for student in UW and also get familiar with design process.

My Role

Heavily involve in all the steps, especially for prototype design and developement by Axure

Design Process

Our project was based on 2013 CHI Student Design Competition design problem, "Changing Perspectives Through Collaboration". From the problem statement, " This year challenge is to design an object, interface, system, or service intended to help us to develop and share awareness, understanding or appreciation for our collective and collaborative crowd experience as it relates to our changing perspectives through collaboration."

Base on the topic, we came out with our design question.

Design question

How can students choose the best food options in and around campus?

Investigation

Base on our design and research questions, invastigation/ user research is conducted by survey, interview, and guided tour(​inspired by IDEO method card). By the end of invastigation stage, primary and secondary personas are built by what we learnt from the researches.

Ideation

Ideation stage starts by making lots of(30+) sketches with who our users in mind. After sketching indivusually and having discussion with team memembers, we evaluate the ideas and combine three of our sketches to come out with one finalized idea which is an iOS application as a solution to our design questions.

Prototyping

To bring the idea closer to reality, the interactive prototype is built up by Axure.

Evaluation

By the prototyping built in prototyping stage, we conduct usability test and evaluate the interaction between our app and users. Finally, redesign some of the pages in the prototype base on the result of usability test.

Documentation

Last but the leat, design specification is written for clarification and as a report for our final project.



Growler Home Security System Reimagination

Introduction

The project is conducted for class HCDE 518, User-centered Design taught by Jess Holbrook. Growler is a comprehensive personal home security system with a unique focus on deterring potential intruders before a home breach occurs.

My Role

I'm heavily involved in all the stages especially interactive prototype building.

Design Constraint

The focus of our team project is to design a physical product.

Design Process
User Research

Survey is desinged and set up on Survey Gizmo for the purpose of collecting relatively large amout of data in short time to give us a sense of people's attitude towards current home security system. After analyzing 100+ response and interview with 7 participants, we build up our primary and secondary personas.

Ideation

Ideate with 30+ scketches and finalize to 2 ideas.

Prototyping

Due to the constraint of building a physical project, we made a low-fi paper prototype by cardboard and an interac tive prototype by axure, a box and an iphone.

Usability Test

The usability test is conducted by our interactive prototype. In the usability test, we provides participants three scenarios and ask them to do a role play with the prototype and share their thoughts with us. With the results/ feedback of our usability test, the product function is redefined.

Documentation

The detailed design is documented in design specification.

Moodlog

Introduction

This project is completed at fall 2011 for class, IMT540: Design Methods for Interactions and Systems.

Topic

Moodlog is an applitcation that empowers users to keep record of their feelings, the place they have been to and analyze the correlation between place and mood. The idea is MoodLog aim to help peopls better understand how their life is affected by the place they were in.

Paper Prototype
Takeaway

I really like the idea. Paper prototype is easy to make and costs little. When making it, it helps me as a designer to refine the function and interaction in the application. Moreover, simply just watch how potential users play with the prototype is a fun and inspiring experience. Some of the interaction problem may still beed to be figured out by Hi-Fi prototype, but in terms of ideation level of usability test, paper prototype really plays its role well.
