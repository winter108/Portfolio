Introduction

Website design project for Tyemill.com with the goal of

  1. Support recruiting efforts.
  2. Prepare for more active participation in the technology community.
  3. Make the website to be easier to mainten.

Resposibility

UX Designer to deliver site structure, wireframes and HTML/CSS.

Design Process
  • Define goal of the project and target users.
  • Conduct Research by interviews and exploration of modern website structures and design.
  • Ideate the site structure and design options.
  • Critique the design with designers and stakeholders
  • Plan for the development timeline.
  • Execute the development plan.
  • Ship the first release with limited features/pages.
