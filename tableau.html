Introduction

Data Visualization project produced during UW CSE 512 Data Visualization Class taught by Prof. Jeff Heer. My goal of the project is to make movie picking experience pleasant and fun using interactive data visualization.


Process
  • Understand the data set.
  • Define goal
  • Data wrangling using excel.
  • Explore various visualizations using tableau.
  • Determine if the visualization can be an interesting storyteller itself.

Deliverable
Movie Tonight?

The data visualization makes experience of picking movie so much easier and fun! Users are able to drill down the selection by picking up a genre first, and then interact with the chart to choose a movie to watch by IMDB rating (how do people like it?), worldwide gross (Is it more of a commercial film or art film?) and the size of the amounts of votes (how much can you trust the rating?)!
