Introduction

Tyemill.com is a Website redesign project I did for Tyemill with the main focus of supporting recruiting efforts and preparingfor more active participation in Seattle technology community.

Responsibility

UX Designer delivered Personas, Information Architecture, Wireframes and HTML/CSS.

Design Process
  • Define goal of the project and target users.
  • Conduct Research by interviews and exploration of modern website structures and design.
  • Ideate site structure and design options.
  • Critique design proposals with designers and stakeholders.
  • Plan for development timeline.
  • Execute development plan.
  • Ship the first release with limited features/pages.
