Introduction

This project was completed in the spring 2013 for COM589: Interactive Design. We aim to design a mobile application which can enhance customers' shopping intention. To be more specific, the goal of the project is to increase CarMax delearship walk-in rate.

My Role

Conduct user interview, protype design and developement by Axure

Design Process

The starting point of the poject is having three primary personas handy provided by the intructor.

User interview:

This is chosen as the first step of the project. The goal of the phase is to know more about our primary users, and figure out how they feel about used car delears. 4 interviews for each persona is conducted. Findings are here.

Competitive Best Practices Audit:

Conduct research about used car dealership's mobile applications in the market to understand our competitors and also prepare for ideation phase.

Ideation:

According to the interview findings and ideas from other mobile applications, the ideation phase starts with LOTS of sketches and comes out with one idea.

Prototyping:

To bring the idea closer to the reality, the prototyping stage starts with interactive prototypoe made by Axure.

User flow analysis:

Instead of doing usability test, in the class, user flow and sitemap is used as tools to analyze the applications.
