Introduction

Data Visualization Research project produced during UW CSE 512 Data Visualization Class taught by Prof. Jeff Heer. The goal of the project is to test the hypothesis that using animation to sequetenially present each part of a simple chart is more effective than showing the whole chart at once.


Process
  • Define the broad research topic: Narrative Data Visualization.
  • Research on related work in the field.
  • Ideate research methodology and structure.
  • Design experiments.
  • Collect and analyze results.
