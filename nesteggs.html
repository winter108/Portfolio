Introduction

Almost every person I know understands the importance of having emergency fund, and they all seem to be saving really hard. You have definitely heard someone around you say, “hey, I’m just gonna have office coffee today, trying to save more.” However, according to Wall street journal, approximately 26% of adults in the United State have no savings prepared for emergencies, while another 36% have yet to start away money for retirement. Why? Why is everyone trying to save, but almost no one can? According to our research, people usually get frustrated with not knowing where to start or not being able to form saving as a habit.
That’s why we came out the idea of Nest Eggs, and app that’s simple, straightforward and help you save money with no pain!!
