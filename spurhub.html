Introduction

Spur Hub is a hackathon project 4 designers put together in 2 days. SpurHub is a direct-action social platform that The goal of SpurHub is to allow individuals to identify and engage with community of shared interest to rapidly and effectively organize online to solve problems they find most important and rise to challenges they find most worthwhile.

Responsibility

I'm the UX Designer of the project and delivered Persona, Information Architecture, Wireframes and HTML/CSS.

Design Process
  • Define goal of the project and target users.
  • Definite MVP scope and goal
  • Conduct Research by interviews and competitor analysis
  • Ideate site structure and design options.
  • Critique design proposals with stakeholders.
