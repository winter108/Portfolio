Introduction

Moodlog is an application that empowers users to keep track of their feelings, the place they have been to and analyze the correlation between places and their mood.


Paper Prototype


Takeaway

Paper prototype is easy to make and costs little. When making it, it helps me as a designer to refine the function and interaction in the application. Moreover, simply just watch how potential users play with the prototype is a fun and inspiring experience. Some of the interaction problem may still need to be figured out by Hi-Fi prototype, but in terms of ideation level of usability test, paper prototype really plays its role well.
