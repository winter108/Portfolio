More About Me

Hello, This is Christine Liu!! I'm currently working at Hewlett-Parker Enterprise as a User Experience Architect focusing on IaaS experience for Cloud Operators. I love being involved in every step of a product. Start with requirements gathering and then translate requirements into product concepts. With the concepts of the product, build up information architecture and the user flows. Then, work on the UX/UI in detail to bring the thoughtfulness into each element of the UI. Finally, party with developers till the day the product gets shipped!


UX Super Power

  1. Design from the conceptual level.
  2. Master complex subject matters quickly.
  3. Gather business, user requirements and further translate them into beautiful interfaces and meaningful interactions.
  4. Identify pain points and build up creative solutions.
  5. Ask great questions and synthesize feedback into design considerations.
