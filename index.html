More About Me (My Resume)

I'm a graduate of University of Washington with a Master degree in Information Management. I love being involved in every step of a product, starting with high level requirements gathering and then translating requirements to product concepts. With the goals of the product, build up information architecture and the basic user flows. Then, work on the UX/UI in detail and bring the thoughtfulness into each part of the product. Finally, work closely with developers until the day the product gets shipped!


UX Super Power

  1. Design from the conceptual level.
  2. Synthesizing users' needs into beautiful interfaces and meaningful interactions.
  3. Identify problems and ideate innovative and professional solutions.
  4. Ask good questions and translate people's questions into design considerations.
