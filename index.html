UWFoody

Introduction

This project was completed in the fall of 2011 for IMT540: Design Methods for Interactions and Systems. The goal of the project is to deliver best food option for student in UW and also get familiar with design process.

My Role

Conduct user interview, protype design and developement by Axure

Design Process

Our project was based on the 2013 CHI Student Design Competition design problem: "Changing Perspectives Through Collaboration". From the problem statement:" This year challenge is to design an object, interface, system, or service intended to help us to develop and share awareness, understanding or appreciation for our collective and collaborative crowd experience as it relates to our changing perspectives through collaboration." Base on the topic, we came out with our design question.

Design question:

How can students choose the best food options in and around campus?

Investigation:

We set up our stakeholders first, and base on our design question to do user researches. Conducting user researches through survey, interview, and guided tour(​inspired by ideo method card). After analyzed the data, redefine our design question and create primary, secondary, supplemental personas and make up scenarios.

Ideation:

Start to ideate by lots of sketches base on our user research.We evaluate our ideas and combine three of our sketches to come out with an iOS application could be the best solution for our design question.

Prototyping:

To bring the idea closer to the reality, the prototyping stage starts with interactive prototypoe made by Axure.

Evaluate

Conduct an usability test and evaluate the interaction between our app and users. Redesign our prototype base on the result of usability test.

Documentation

See the project specification

My Role

I'm heavily involved in every stage especially for building interactive prototype.

Design contraint

The focus of our team is to create a physical product

Design Process
User Research:

Conduct Survey with 100+ response and interview with 7 participants from survey. Create primary and secondary persona.

Ideation:

Ideate with 30+ scketches and finalize by 2 ideas.

Prototyping:

Due to the characterist of our project, we made a low-fi paper prototype and a interactive prototype by axure, a box and an iphone.

Usability Test:

The usability test is conducted by our interactive prototype which asked the participant to do a role play with it.

Documentation:

The detailed design is documented in the spec.
