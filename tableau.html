Introduction

Data Visualization project produced during UW CSE 512 Data Visualization Class by Prof. Jeff Heer. The goal of the project is to make a useful data visualization using data provided by the professor.


Process
  • Understand the data set.
  • Ideate using tableau and explore the data.
  • Deteremine if the visualization can be an interesting story talker.

Deliverable
What do you want to watch tonight?

The data visualization make experience of picking movie so much easier and fun! Users are able to drill down the selection by deciding a genre first, and then make their final decision by IMDB rating (how do people like it?), production budget (how "big" of a film is it?) and the size of the amounts of votes (Credibility of the rating)!
