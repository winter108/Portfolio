Introduction

Website redesign project for University of Washington Data and Analytics team. Goal of the project is to

  1. Build up a refreshing brand image for the team
  2. Reconstruct the site structure and make things easy to be found.

Resposibility

UX and Visual Designer of the project working with two other developers.

Design Process
  • Define goal of the proejct and gather business and user requirements from the stakeholders .
  • Research on current user groups and the site structure.
  • Ideate information archetecture and design options.
  • Critique internally with team members and externally with the clients.
  • Plan for the development timeline and priority of features.
  • Execute the design and developmenet plan.
